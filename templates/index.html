Welcome to my "blog". I've always hated the word blog, it sounds dumb - feels like a 2008 disney show type thing. Not sure what else to call it though - so for now, it's my blog. I also can't say i'll be keeping up this stupid official tone, I'm not writing a script for some show, I am writing my thoughts and what I've been doing lately, leading from that, I should explain what my blog is for.

Mainly, it's so I can keep updates of my life and my programming projects. Mainly my programming though, I'm not creating a blog from the ground up to discuss how much i like long walks on the beach, I hope I'm not that vain. I'll be updating this hopefully every other day, with current projects I'm doing, along with things I've learnt. Perhaps I'll even write a post about something super cool I found out occasionaly, in the hopes someone might make use of it.

I think that describes it well. Feel free to check out my portfolio website or my github profile. Enjoy :)
